Governance, Risk Management, and Compliance Service

Governance, risk management, and compliance (GRC) refers to the activities needed to accomplish these three interrelated functions. Aarohi’s certified consultants are experienced in understanding your requirements, assisting you in developing your GRC strategy, and then implementing and operating it. Failures in these critical functions can be fatal to the business, as witnessed by the implosions of Enron and Lehman Brothers among others. Successfully implementing and operating GRC means that you are:

  • Operating the corporation in alignment with the directives of the Board of Directors and Executives,
  • Maintaining an effective assessment of existing and emerging risks and successfully eliminating or mitigating them, and;
  • Meeting all of the requirements of governmental regulations.

Correctly instituting and performing these functions will allow you to meet the demands of internal and external audits thereby demonstrating that the corporation is meeting its obligations to its stakeholders.

The Services we provide under our GRC umbrella are:

Regulatory Compliance Assessment for Sarbanes Oxley (SOX), FDA, GLBA, etc.
Standards Analysis for ISO 27000 (information security management)
Strategy and Plans
Solution Design
Technology Configurations
Implementation
Operation and maintenance
Ongoing reassessment and evaluation
